About
Hi! I'm Betty, a second-year student at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science and Cognitive Science. College life can be overwhelming, and the days tend to blend into each other as we start measuring the passage of time by deadlines instead of by days of the week. This semester, I've made a point to take pictures of almost everything I eat, as an effort to avoid getting swept up in the whirlwind of midterms and projects. I have always found food to be one of the greatest joys in life. These are some of the things I've eaten this spring; in many ways, these meals have been the heartbeat of my semester. I hope they bring you joy, too.
